Frequently Asked Questions

Event Information

Do I need to register before attending an event?

Yes. Early registration helps us plan seating, catering, event materials, and accommodation where applicable.

Can family members attend?

Some events are family-friendly, while others are exclusive to registered alumni. Details are provided on each event page.

How can I volunteer?

Contact the Events Committee through the Contact page or indicate your interest during event registration.
