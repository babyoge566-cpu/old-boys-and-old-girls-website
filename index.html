Welcome Message

Message from the Alumni President

Welcome to the Legacy Heritage Alumni Association. Our association exists to preserve the proud traditions of our beloved school while creating opportunities for alumni across the globe to reconnect, mentor students, support development projects and celebrate lifelong friendships.

Together we continue building a legacy of leadership, excellence, integrity and service for future generations.
