Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to a project?

Donations can be made through our secure online donation portal or during official alumni fundraising events.

Can I sponsor a specific project?

Yes. Sponsors may choose to support individual projects, scholarship funds, or infrastructure initiatives.

Are volunteers welcome?

Absolutely. Alumni can volunteer their professional skills, time, and resources to support ongoing projects.
