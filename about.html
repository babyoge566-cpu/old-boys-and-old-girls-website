About Our Association

Building Lifelong Connections,
Inspiring Future Generations

Legacy Heritage Alumni Association is a vibrant community of former students committed to preserving our school's traditions, strengthening friendships, supporting educational excellence, and giving back to the next generation.

Who We Are

A Family That Never Graduates

Legacy Heritage Alumni Association exists to unite graduates across different generations and geographical locations. We believe that the friendships formed within our school walls extend far beyond graduation, creating opportunities for mentorship, networking, lifelong learning, and meaningful community service.

Through annual reunions, educational support programmes, scholarship initiatives, professional networking, and charitable outreach, we continue to uphold the values that shaped every Legacy Heritage student.

Our Journey

Our History

Since its formation, the Legacy Heritage Alumni Association has remained committed to preserving the traditions of our beloved school while empowering graduates to make meaningful contributions to society.

1985

Foundation

The association was established to reconnect graduates and preserve lifelong friendships after leaving school.

1998

First National Reunion

Hundreds of alumni gathered for the first nationwide reunion, establishing annual traditions that continue today.

2012

Scholarship Programme

Alumni launched a scholarship initiative to support brilliant students from financially disadvantaged backgrounds.

Today

Global Network

Our members now span several continents, supporting one another professionally, socially and philanthropically.

Our Identity

Motto & School Anthem

The values of Legacy Heritage continue to inspire every graduate, reminding us that education extends beyond the classroom and lasts a lifetime.

School Motto

"Knowledge • Character • Service"

Our motto reflects our commitment to academic excellence, moral integrity, servant leadership, and lifelong learning. Every graduate is encouraged to uphold these principles throughout life.

School Anthem

Legacy Heritage, ever shining,
Home of honour, truth and light;
May our knowledge keep us striving,
Guiding every future flight.

Bound together through our learning,
Strong in friendship, brave and true;
May our service bless our nation,
As we proudly honour you.
